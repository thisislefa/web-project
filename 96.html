Frequently Asked Questions

Have more questions? We've got answers.

We primarily offer Infrastructure-as-a-Service (IaaS) with products like Virtual Machines and Block Storage, and Platform-as-a-Service (PaaS) with our Managed Kubernetes and Managed Databases. This gives you the most flexibility and control, while we manage the underlying hardware and platform stability.

Security is our foundation. All data is encrypted at rest (AES-256) and in-transit (TLS 1.2+). Our data centers are certified for SOC 2, ISO 27001, and we provide tools to help you maintain HIPAA and PCI-DSS compliance. Our AI-powered security center monitors for threats 24/7.

Our migrations are planned in 4 phases: Discover, Architect, Migrate, and Optimize. We use a "lift-and-shift" or "re-platform" approach based on your needs. For critical applications, we plan for parallel environments to ensure zero downtime during the final cutover.

For services like Object Storage and Serverless, you are billed only for what you use, calculated per-hour or per-GB. For IaaS products like VMs, you are billed hourly for the resources you provision, whether they are running or stopped. You can see a real-time cost breakdown in your control panel.
